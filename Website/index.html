WHAT
IS
SURF

Surf is a genre of music that doesn't ring a bell to a lot of people. But for some it is the only genre of music. Surf music is a subgenre of rock that was created around the '70 by the surf culture and surf people. Mainly because thet wanted to people feel the rhythm of the sea and sound of the waves trough music. You might think that surf is dead. But we are a great example that surf music is very lively in different comunities around the world.
