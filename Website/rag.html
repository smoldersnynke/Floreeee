What is ryhtm and glow?

Ryhtm and glow is an event made by surfers for surfers. We have a great surf competition with some of the best surfers in the country. We have a different price pool for everbody. We provide great food for a reasonable price with fresh ingredients. We also showcase our new artist from flo'ryde. Our artist will guarantee for a wild night and for a party that you will remember for a long time.
